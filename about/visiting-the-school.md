## Security

Any visitor is required to sign in at the security desk and to show valid photo ID. No one will be allowed into the school without first signing in with security.

## Directions

Our school is located on East 82nd Street between First and Second Avenues on the Upper East Side. Visitors may enter the building through the **single red door that is closest to First Avenue**.

Closest subway stops are the 4/5/6 at East 86th Street or the Q at East 86th Street.

## School Tours

School tours for prospective families will be offered in December, January, and February. For details see [Enrollment](/about/enrollment/).

**When we do offer tours:**

- Participation requires prior registration plus confirmation from the school.
- Tours are for adults only.
- Tours last about 45 minutes.
- All participants must bring valid photo ID (driver's license, NY State ID, or passport).
- No beverages may be brought into the building.
