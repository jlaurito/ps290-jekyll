## Required Documents for Each Student, Every Year

**MNS Families:**

⭑ Make sure you have sent in your **Emergency Contact Form (below)**! This is required for every student each year.

- [Emergency Contact Form](https://4.files.edl.io/678e/02/20/25/185906-88a48e13-1040-4b0d-a673-eff6afd0a818.pdf)

⭑ Be sure to complete your [School Lunch Form](https://www.myschoolapps.com/Home/PickDistrict) each year, regardless of whether your child has school lunch or brings their own. This impacts school funding. You're not required to provide any personal information.

⭑ **Medication:** If your child has allergies, asthma, or might require medication during school, you must submit a completed Medication Administration Form (MAF) from your doctor **every year** before school starts. All forms and translations can be found on the [DOE health services page](https://www.schools.nyc.gov/school-life/health-and-wellness/health-services).

- [Allergy MAF](https://4.files.edl.io/753d/02/20/25/185956-cc45eba7-e0f4-4dac-b466-9cbc15b305b0.pdf)
- [Asthma MAF](https://4.files.edl.io/1525/02/20/25/185956-bf0509f6-4374-4bcc-b11f-d69eb9691c2e.pdf)
- [General Medicine MAF](https://4.files.edl.io/aa95/02/20/25/185956-6913145f-ac6b-4334-a1a4-5a9e1e679dc0.pdf)

All parents/guardians are encouraged to read the following:

- [Non-Discrimination Policy](https://www.schools.nyc.gov/about-us/policies/non-discrimination-policy) (translations are also featured)
  - [Notice of Non-Discrimination Under Section 504](https://4.files.edl.io/aa20/02/20/25/190029-1fdb359c-1cfa-47be-b793-13d42d4a2521.pdf)
